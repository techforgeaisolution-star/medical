About DiseasePredict

A machine learning powered health risk assessment platform built by final-year MCA students.

🎯 Our Mission

DiseasePredict is our major final-year project aimed at revolutionizing early disease detection. By leveraging trained machine learning models, we help users assess their risk for common diseases and encourage timely medical consultation — making preventive healthcare accessible to everyone.

MCA students, we combine our strong foundation in computer applications and software engineering to build intelligent, real-world solutions that bridge technology and healthcare.

✨ Project Highlights
🤖ML-powered predictions with trained clinical models
🩸Diabetes risk assessment (8 clinical parameters)
❤️Heart disease evaluation (13 cardiac parameters)
📊Real-time risk gauge and probability distribution
📄Downloadable PDF health report
📱Fully responsive across all devices

🏫 About the Team

We are a passionate team of final-year Master of Computer Applications (MCA) students dedicated to building intelligent solutions for healthcare. This project was developed as part of our major project submission for the Department of Computer Applications.

⚠️ DiseasePredict is built for educational purposes only. The predictions generated by this tool are based on machine learning models and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
